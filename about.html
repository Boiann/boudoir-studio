Frequently Asked Questions

Is Boudoir Photograpy Expensive?
We have many packages suited for different budgets. We can do a small and short photoshoot (starting at Eur 200) to almost full-day experience in one of our hotel partners (up to Eur 1000). The best thing is to get in contact with us so we can discuss everything!
What if I'm not confident in my looks? I'm no model...
Join the crowd! All of the women we take through the Boudoir Experience think they cannot amount to the "beautiful" standard. The goal of the experience is to prove to you that you are beautiful, unique and special because of who you are. At the end of the experience it is our promise you will feel like a model!
Will other people be able to see my pictures?
Privacy is something we take very seriously. At the start of the photoshoot we will provide you with a document/contract where you will be able to choose if the pictures are for your eyes only (or for someone special), or can they be used in our website. Most women are so satisfied that they want the world to see the images!
Do you only shoot in your studio?
No! We are flexible in terms of shoot location. Our studio is set up so there is a lot of natural light coming in and the furniture is minimalistic. But we have also partnered with some of the hotels in Wexford area so we can shoot in their rooms, or better yet, absolutely exquisite Penthouse Suites! There is also an option of outside shoot (rain, temperature and sunshine dependant).
Can I bring someone to the shoot?
Absolutely! Having your friends or loved ones see you in a different light will go a long way to boost your confidence. The only thing we wouldn't reccomend is bringing your children - that is always a bit awkward. And if the kids are younger, it leads to a lot of giggles!
How will I be able to relax in front of a male photographer?
Some women actually prefer male photographer, but we understand if you feel uneasy. What we can promise if relaxed and fun environment (there's chocolate and sparkling wine). Bryson (our photographer) is very professional and knows how to direct posing with ease. The choice of clothing, amount of make-up (we recommend minimal) are entirely up to you!
