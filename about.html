Bryson Sueai

Photographer

Bryson was born and raised in Irelans, Wexford. After travelling the globe post college (diploma in Photography Art), he found his true passion: Boudoir. He has been empowering women for the last 12 years, giving them a chance to understand just how beautiful they are. Opening his own studio in his hometown is a dream come true.

Anda Soee

Make-up Artist

Anda was nominated top-tier make up artist last year in her hometown of Johannesburg. She came to Ireland for a holiday where she met Bryson and the rest is as how they say, history. Their partnership is based on mutual respect and love towards the Boudoir art and what it represents.

Minoa Soffh

Hair Stylist

Minoa comes as the last (but not the least) addition to the team from Sydney, Australia. Her experience working on movie sets translated well to Boudoir. She can apply her knowledge of subtle make-up, just enough, not to cover and hide but to bring forward your natural beauty.
